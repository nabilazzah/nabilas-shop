How to request personal commission

1. Contact me
You can contact me via Email: raraenabila@gmail.com
2. Quality photos
The watercolour potrait based on your provided personal photos. The best reference photos are medium to high resolutin, in clear focus. I will do my best to work with the photo of your choice, but may reject reference photos that are too small or unclear
3. Any whim
I can also combine separate reference photos of different people or pets into one painting. Extraneous backgrounds can be replaced with an expressive wash in color(s) of your coice, or an interpretation of the existing environment can be included.
4. Payment
I work with 60% prepayment before pencil sketch and 40% after pencil sketch but before colouring. Payment can be made via GoPay. I won't start work in colours until you confirm the sketch.
5. Price details
This prices list is only guideline and final price can be different because of background and details. Special requests also may be priced differently, to be discussed before a deposit is made. Custom sizes are also available.
